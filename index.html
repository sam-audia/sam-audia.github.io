About the Challenge

Who

This challenge is for beginners and hobbyists looking to practice CADing, slicing, and printing objects out of their plastic of choice.

What

This is a six week challenge where you will design and print a new part every week. To keep you motivated we have created different paths for you to explore different aspects of 3D printing. There are more lessons than their are weeks so feel free to run this challenge multiple.

How

Scroll down and you'll find that page is divided into threee sections. These are your available paths covering three different aspects of 3D printing. Stick to one path or mix it up. The only rules are that you must do the first lesson and you must create a print every week.
